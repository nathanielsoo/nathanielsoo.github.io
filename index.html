Orthogonal Portfolio Construction with Random Walk Analysis

Python, Web Scraping, Random Walk Analysis

By modelling the covariance between different assets, assets that are largely uncorrelated with one another are identified and added to the portfolio according to automatically adjusted weights, based on individual asset's historical performance.
The portfolio automatically rebalances in order to methodically reduce idiosyncratic risk without a proportional decline in returns.
To further improve the robustness of risk mitigation, Walk Forward Analysis was conducted on additional pseudorandom generated data, based on the distribution of past returns. Based on the outcomes of the many simulations, the probability distribution of returns can be obtained, with complementary VaR analysis.
